Can the account support the trade

  • Options level
  • Equity Requirement
    • $20,000 for spreads
    • $50,000 for index spreads
    • $5,000 cash spread reserve in IRA
  • Cash/Margin needed for Trade
    • Option Requirements (OneView Tools)
  • Adjusted Options Cues and Clues
    • Cannot trade on the Web
    • Premiums do not make sense
    • Additional values in quotes or readback
    • and extra numerical digit before the yr on the symbol
  • Trading hours
    • Equity and narrow based index options
      • 9:30 am - 4:00pm EST
    • Broad based index options
      • 9:30 am - 4:15pm EST

Trade Approval

  • Opening Trades
  • >50 contracts
  • >15 uncovered
  • No approval required for closing trades
  • Trades larger than 500k
