Margin Examples

Margin Equity Calculation

  • Total all Margin credits and debits to calculate Margin Equity
    • Margin Equity = Margin Mkt Value – Margin DB – Short Mkt Value + Sht Cr Bal
    • $594,925 = $747,525 - $162,859.26 - $244,950 + $255,210.24

  • Subtract the Security Requirements of the account to get the House Surplus
    • House Surplus = Margin Equity - Security Reqts
    • $31,285 = $594,925 - $563,640

House Driver

  • Look at NON-MGN Security. What combination will likely get the non-mgn security between House and Surplus if we add in the cash?
    • >NON-MGN Security = Net Cash (Total Core Mny Mkts - cash DB BAL) + House Surplus
    • $9,084 = $8,635 + $448

  • Because the house surplus + cash equaled the non-mgn security, the House Surplus is the driver

  • We can then determine that the Cash/Margin by: NON-MGN Security/House Requirement
    • $30,280 = $9,084 / 30%

SMA/Fed Driver

  • Look at NON-MGN Security and compare that number with the House Surplus + Cash
    • $110,702 ≠ $110,705 + $0
  • Because NON-MGN Security does not equal the House Surplus + Cash, we conclude the SMA is the driver
  • Confirm the SMA is the driver by: Cash/Margin = SMA / Fed Requirement
    • $237,063 = $118,534.34 / 50%

Limited Margin (Overnight Buying power)

  • Overnight Buying Power = Total Core Mny Mkts*(check net cash) + Margin CR Balance
    • $52,254 = $4,647.33 + $47607.53

Day Trade Buying Power (no restrictions)

  • Day Tr Buying Power = Start of Day NYSE Surplus * 4 (No Account Restriction)
    • $21,879.28 = $5469 * 4

Intraday Buying Power (Current buying power)

  • On the BSUM Screen (F8), enter on the NEXT: "FB"
  • New Buying Power = ( Total Core Mny Mkts + NYSE Surplus ) * $ (unrestricted account)
    • $960,739.92 = ( $128,020.98 + $112.164 ) * 4
