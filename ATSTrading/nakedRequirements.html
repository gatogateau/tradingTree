Naked Options Requirements

    Equity Calls

  • The higher of these requirements
    • 25% of the underlying stock value - the out of the money amount + the premium
    • 15% of the underlying stock value + the premium

    ______________

    Equity Puts

  • The higher of these requirements
    • 25% of the underlying stock value - the out of the money amount + the premium
    • premium + 15% of the strike price (for both in the money and out of the money)

    ______________

    Uncovered Equity and Index Straddles

  • The greater of:
    • The two uncovered requirements + the premium on the other option, per straddle

    ______________

    Equity and Index Spreads

    For Credit Spreads
  • The lesser of:
    • The uncovered margin requirements on the short option
    • The difference between strikes

    ______________

  • **For debit spreads: requirement is the amount of the debit
